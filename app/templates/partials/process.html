OUR design PROCESS

Here's how our typical business process works with new customers:

Client Meeting
Delivery
1

Gather requirements. Understand purpose of the system. Set goals and determine direction.

2

Research. Gather tools and work force.

3

Design prototypes and reiterate. Test and compare different prototypes and gather feedback.

4

Develop the product. Add functionality to the design.

5

Test. Stage the product for clients to use and provide feedback.
